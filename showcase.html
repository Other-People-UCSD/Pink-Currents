ABOUT US

Other People Literary Magazine is UC San Diego's first student-run literary magazine and club. We publish biannually in the Fall and Spring, and accept works of fiction, poetry, creative nonfiction, art, photography, and other artistic mediums. We wish to present the artistic capabilities in UCSD students through an inclusive platform to distribute creative works, and to encourage literary and artistic expression among all students. We celebrate our diversity, our individual uniqueness, our shared experiences, and our collective journey through this world. Check out our issues below!
