ABOUT US

Other People Literary Magazine at UC San Diego is the first student-run literary magazine and club. We publish biannually in the Fall and Spring, and accept works of fiction, poetry, creative nonfiction, art, photography, and other artistic mediums. We wish to present the artistic capabilities in UCSD students through an inclusive platform to distribute creative works, and to encourage literary and artistic expression among all students. We celebrate our diversity, our individual uniqueness, our shared experiences, and our collective journey through this world. Check out our issues below!

Team

Our team consists of writers, artists, and even STEM undergraduates! We come from a diverse set of backgrounds and majors, so if you're interested in joining an awesome team of creative enthusiasts please fill out the respective application forms below!

  • Editorial & Content

    Editors review and edit submissions, then produce the pages of the magazine. Content writers create exclusive prose, interview submittors about their stories, and may write articles of their choice.

    (Apply Here!)

  • Design & Social Media

    Design illustrators create artwork to complement accepted prose. We publish digital and print copies of issues, as well as designing inclusive access to artistic expression through our website.
