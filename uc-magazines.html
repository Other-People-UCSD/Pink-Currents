List of UC Creative Magazines & Journals

The University of California system has a multitude of magazines and journals highlighting the creative minds of their students. Yet none of the UC websites contain a collective listing of these magazines, so it is our duty as a student-led magazine to help students find their way to the pages across our UC system.

To include your own description, logo, expected submissions timeline & who can submit, email us at otherpeopleucsd@gmail.com!
